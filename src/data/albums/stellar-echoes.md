**Stellar Echoes** is the second album by synthwave artist Echo Lume. Building on the success of _Neon Dreams_, this album explores cosmic themes with haunting synths, pulsating beats, and cinematic soundscapes. Tracks like _Neon Voyage_ and _Starlight Symphony_ take listeners on an intergalactic journey through the dreamy landscapes of synthwave.
